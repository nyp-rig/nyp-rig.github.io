Our Epic Projects

🦾

Prosthetic Arm Project

We're designing 3D-printed prosthetic arms controlled by muscle sensors. This project could change lives and is competing in the National Innovation Challenge!

🌱

Smart Greenhouse System

An AI-powered greenhouse that monitors plants, controls watering, and optimizes growing conditions. It's like having a green thumb, but with code!

🚗

Autonomous Racing Car

Our self-driving mini race car uses computer vision and machine learning to navigate tracks at lightning speed. We're gunning for the championship!

🎵

DJ Robot Performer

This robot doesn't just play music - it creates beats, responds to crowd energy, and puts on light shows. It's been featured at school events!

🏠

Smart Home Assistant

Voice-controlled home automation with facial recognition, mood lighting, and energy optimization. The future of smart living, built by students!

⚽

Soccer Robot League

Robots that play soccer with strategy, teamwork, and precision. We compete internationally and consistently rank in the top 5 globally!

Ready to Join the Future?

Whether you're a coding newbie or a tech wizard, there's a place for you in our robotics family!

🎓 Skill Level: Any

Never touched code? Perfect! Started building robots in kindergarten? Even better! We welcome all skill levels and will help you grow.

⏰ Time Commitment

2-3 sessions per week, 2 hours each. Plus awesome weekend workshops, competitions, and optional hackathons for the truly obsessed!

💰 What's Included

All equipment provided! Arduino kits, sensors, 3D printer access, competition fees covered, plus free pizza during late-night build sessions.
