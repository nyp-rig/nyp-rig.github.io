Building

Ready to push the boundaries of innovations? Join us and build some amazing robots with us! Here you can master VEX, Arduino, Microbit, and learn common contraptions like a scissor lift, pneumatics systems and electrical engineering. From beginners to pros, everyone's welcome.

Programming

Be part of the ever-evloving society of programmers who are constantly pushing the hardware limits of our robots. Learn complex theories like odometry and autonomous programming through the use of Python, JavaScript and C++. We also offer block programming for beginners.
